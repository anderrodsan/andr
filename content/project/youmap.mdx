## Description

An interactive map to explore your favorite youtuber videos!

<ScreenShots id="youmap" />

## Key Features

- **Discover Channels:** See the list of youtubers available in the platform.
- **Map:** Explore the interactive map to find out where the videos were recorded, or find videos of a specific region.

## Frameworks & Languages

These are the frameworks and languages I used to develop the dashboard.

- **Nextjs**
- **React**
- **Tailwind CSS (Nativewind)**
- **Typescript**
- **Shadcn UI**

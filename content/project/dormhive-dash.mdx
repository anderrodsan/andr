<h3>Description</h3>

Simplify administrative tasks, boost communication, and create a vibrant community atmosphere in your dormitory with DormHive's innovative platform.

<ScreenShots id="dormhive-dash" />

<hr />

<h3>Key Features</h3>

- **Dashboard:** Get occupancy stats, overview of the main functionalities.
- **User Management:** Add/remove users easily, asigning their room number for an easy management and filtering.
- **Tasks:** Get messages from the tenants and create tasks for staff members.
- **Group Management:** Create/remove groups and assign users based on their attributes.
- **Customize Tenants App:** Add custom description, Q&A, rules, facility lists, etc.
